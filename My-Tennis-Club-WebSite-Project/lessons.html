CHILDREN’S GROUPS

It is a training program organized to provide skill and coordination development to children between the ages of 4-16, and to bring players to our academy by teaching and loving tennis. Our program, which starts with the school term, serves between September 1st and June 1st. Successful children transition to infrastructure under the supervision of our trainers.

ADULT GROUPS

It is a program that includes technical-tactical and conditioning training by our trainers for adult players of all levels.

    • ABOUT US

    Our academy, which is headquartered in Kayseri and has a branch in Talas, offers high-level sports education to our students with its fully equipped facilities and experienced trainer staff. Our goal is; It is to guide our students in their sports careers by bringing the most correct solution suggestions to different expectations and to bring a new breath to Turkish tennis.
    Our Club is founded in 1965 by former Turkish ATP pro tennis player Okan Topdemir.
